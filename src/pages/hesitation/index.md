Your remarkable intelligence...

Test and unmatched.

It's hard to believe I'm awake...

Wise and strong from front to back.

Be mine, for goodness sakes!

So beautiful and so lovely,

The sky would weep.

It is for these reasons that I hesitate to sleep.
