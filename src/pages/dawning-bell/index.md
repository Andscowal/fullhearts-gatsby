Gong, gong, gong, the dawning bell's song.

Billows and bellows, it won't ring for long.

Dreams as big as it's soulful voice.

Love springs eternal above all noise.

To love you forever, nothing can go wrong.

Gong, gong, gong, the dawning bell's song.
